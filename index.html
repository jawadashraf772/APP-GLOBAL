Answers for Technical Buyers

What teams typically ask before engaging us — from delivery timelines to support and pricing.

What types of applications do you develop?

We specialize in crafting web, mobile, and desktop applications, including custom CRM, HRM, and RSM systems. Our solutions are tailored to your business needs, ensuring scalability, user-focused design, and seamless performance across platforms.

How long does it take to develop a custom application?

Development timelines depend entirely on the complexity and scope of the project. Generally, an initial MVP takes between 8 to 12 weeks, with complete enterprise systems taking 4-6 months.

Do you offer support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive SLA-backed 24/7 support and maintenance packages, ensuring your application remains secure, up-to-date, and fully operational.

Can you modernize or upgrade my existing application?

Yes, we specialize in legacy modernization. We can refactor existing codebases, migrate platforms to the cloud, or rebuild the frontend to improve UX and performance.

What is included in your software development services?

Our end-to-end services include business analysis, UI/UX design, architecture planning, frontend & backend development, QA testing, deployment, and ongoing post-launch support.

How much does it cost to develop a custom application?

Costs vary based on the feature set, integrations, and platform choices. After an initial discovery call, we provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing and milestone breakdowns.
